What are the key responsibilities of a solo tester in a small team?
Helps to be highly adaptable with multiple test types.
Often coaching on quality practices to multiple teams.
One person can be liason to multiple teams.
Often manage entire quality strategy.

How does being part of an cross-functional team change the tester's role?
Requires close real time collaboration for continuous testing.
More of a focus on Shift Left.
Often found in Agile team.
**Although I personally have had experiences where I was integrated but still had
to operate within a testing-last almost waterfall approach.

When might an organisation use crowd testing?
To ask security specialists to test their resilience against cyber attacks (black 
box) or monitor application performance (white box).
